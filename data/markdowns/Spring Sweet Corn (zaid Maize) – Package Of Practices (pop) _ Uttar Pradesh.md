# Spring Sweet Corn (Zaid Maize) – Package of Practices (POP)

## 1. Crop Overview

* **Crop**: Sweet Corn (*Zea mays* var. *saccharata*)
* **Season**: Spring / Zaid
* **Duration**: 75–90 days
* **Suitable Region**: Western, Central & Eastern Uttar Pradesh with assured irrigation

## 2. Climate & Soil Requirement

Close-up of a corn ear with yellow kernels and green husks

* **Temperature**: 18–30°C
* **Rainfall**: Low rainfall preferred; grown under irrigation

* **Soil**:
  * Well-drained loam to sandy loam
  * pH: 6.0–7.5
  * Avoid waterlogging

## 3. Recommended Varieties / Hybrids

**Sweet Corn Hybrids (Spring Season)**

* Madhuri (IIMR)

* Sugar-75

*(Select variety based on seed availability & local market demand)*

## 4. Land Preparation

* One deep ploughing followed by 2–3 harrowings

* Proper leveling for uniform irrigation

* **Apply FYM**: 4–6 tons/acre during last ploughing

## 5. Sowing Details

* **Sowing Time**: 15 January – 20 February

* **Seed Rate**: 3–4 kg/acre

* **Spacing**:

- Row to row: 60 cm

- Plant to plant: 20 cm

* **Method**: Line sowing / ridge & furrow

* **Seed Treatment**:

- Thiram or Carbendazim @ 2 g/kg seed

- Biofertilizer: Azotobacter + PSB

## 6. Nutrient Management (per acre)

**Recommended Dose**: 50:25:17 kg N:P:K / acre

* **Basal Application**:

- Full P & K + 1/3 Nitrogen at sowing

* **Top Dressing**:

- 1/3 Nitrogen at knee-high stage (20–25 DAS)

- 1/3 Nitrogen at tasseling stage (40–45 DAS)

**Micronutrient**

* Zinc Sulphate @ 10 kg/acre (once in 2–3 years)

## 7. Irrigation Management

* First irrigation immediately after sowing

Sweet corn plant with developing ears

* Subsequent irrigations at:

- Knee-high stage

- Tasseling

- Silking

- Grain filling

* **Total**: 6–8 light irrigations

* Avoid moisture stress during tasseling & silking

## 8. Weed Management

* **Critical Period**: 15–45 DAS
* **Pre-emergence**:
  - Atrazine @ **400 g a.i./acre** within 2 days of sowing
* **Mechanical**:
  - One hand weeding at 25–30 DAS

## 9. Pest Management

### Major Pests

* **Stem Borer**

- Trichogramma release: **20,000/acre**

- Chlorantraniliprole 18.5 SC @ **60 ml/acre**

* **Fall Armyworm** (if observed)

- Emamectin benzoate 5 SG @ **0.4 g/litre of water**

## 10. Disease Management

* **Leaf Blight / Rust**

- Mancozeb @ **2.5 g/litre**

* **Downy Mildew**

- Metalaxyl + Mancozeb @ **2 g/litre**

## 11. Harvesting

* Harvest at **milk stage** (18–22 days after silking)

* Silk turns brown, husk remains green

* Early morning harvest preferred for better sweetness

## 12. Yield (per acre)

* **Green Cob Yield**: 5–6 tons/acre

* **Green Fodder Yield**: 8–10 tons/acre
